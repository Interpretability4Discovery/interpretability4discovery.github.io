Formatting & LaTeX

All submissions must be formatted using the Interp4Discovery modified NeurIPS LaTeX style. Submissions are limited to the page counts specified above. There is no limit on appendices or references, but reviewers are not required to read them.

Policies

visibility_off

Double-blind Review

Submissions must be anonymized. Do not include author names, affiliations, or identifying information in the initial PDF. Cite your own previous work in the third person.

inventory_2

Non-Archival

The workshop is non-archival. Accepted papers will be posted on the workshop website, but this does not preclude future submission to journals or conferences. We welcome work recently accepted or under review elsewhere.

verified_user

Responsible Use Statement

Authors must include a brief statement in the appendix regarding the responsible use of interpretability tools, ensuring that mechanistic insights are not misinterpreted as causal certainties without appropriate validation.
